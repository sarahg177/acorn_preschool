Welcome to
Acorn Pre-School

WHAPLODE

Acorn Pre-School is a committee run setting located within the grounds of Whaplode C of E Primary School in Whaplode, Lincolnshire

We offer childcare for children between the ages of two and five years old. We have a team of highly trained and qualified childcare professionals dedicated to giving each and every child a great start before beginning primary school. We offer the appropriate ratios to allow them personal one to one opportunities to develop social and education skills. Each child leaves us and moves on to primary school with literacy, numeracy and other core skills Skills such as identifying colours and shapes to ensure they are in the best position possible furthering their education.
